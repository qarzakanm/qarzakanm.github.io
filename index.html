Privacy Policy

Qarzakanm ("the App") is developed and provided by Dyari Nasih ("we," "our," or "us"). We value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

- The App primarily works offline and stores your debts and financial records locally on your device. - Optionally, users may create an account through Firebase Authentication to unlock additional features. - We do not collect sensitive personal information such as your location, contacts, or device files.

2. How We Use Your Data

- Your financial records are saved only on your device’s local storage. - If you create an account, your email and authentication details are managed securely through Firebase. - We do not share, sell, or trade your personal information with third parties.

3. Data Backup

- The App allows you to back up your data locally on your device storage. - No automatic cloud backup is performed unless you explicitly use Firebase login features.

4. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions: - INTERNET and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Required only for Firebase account login and syncing subscription status. No other device permissions are used.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, since most data is stored offline on your device, it is your responsibility to secure your phone and backups.
