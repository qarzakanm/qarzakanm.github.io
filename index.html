Privacy Policy

Qarzakanm. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

- The App primarily works offline and stores your debts and financial records locally on your device. - If you create an account, we collect your email address and password through Firebase Authentication to enable login and syncing of your subscription status. - We do not collect sensitive data such as your contacts, location, or files outside the App.

2. How We Use Your Data

- Your financial records are saved only on your device and are not uploaded to our servers. - Your email and authentication details are processed securely by Firebase Authentication (a Google service). - We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to provide the Firebase login service.

3. Data Backup

- The App allows you to back up your data locally on your device. - No automatic cloud backup is performed unless you log in with Firebase Authentication, in which case only your account details (email) are stored in Firebase.

4. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions: - INTERNET and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: required only for Firebase Authentication and subscription verification. No other device permissions are requested.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Since most data is stored offline on your device, you are responsible for securing your phone and backups.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover such data has been provided, we will delete it immediately.
